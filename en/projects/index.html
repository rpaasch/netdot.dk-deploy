Projects

Professional Projects

Here is a selection of projects I’ve worked on throughout my career.

T4VIS-In3D (Trainers for visually impaired students introduce 3D printing)

International Project Management | Erasmus+

As project manager on this Erasmus+ project, I worked with an international team to develop tactile 3D-printed teaching materials for the blind. The project focused on providing educators with tools and methods to create accessible learning materials through additive manufacturing.

VR4VIP (Virtual Reality for Visually Impaired People)

Innovation | Accessibility

Research and development project exploring the possibilities of using Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR) for education and rehabilitation of visually impaired citizens. The project combines cutting-edge technology with special educational insights.

FysikKemifaget.dk (Physics/Chemistry Subject)

Digital Learning Materials | Editorial Work

As a publishing editor at Clio Online, I was responsible for developing this complete digital educational resource for primary and lower secondary schools. The project included strategy development, financial management, daily editing, and cross-editorial projects.

Digital Agency (Rasborg & Paasch)

Web Design | Custom Functionality

Partnership in a digital agency focusing on developing websites with customized functionality primarily for the education sector. Our projects ranged from consulting to design and development for clients such as Alinea and Smagens Dag.

STEM Education

Pedagogical Development | Technology in Education

Development and implementation of STEM subjects at Langelinieskolen with a focus on innovation, coding, design processes, entrepreneurship, and creative projects within science. The project integrated practical problem-solving with digital tools.

Current Projects

Web Accessibility (WCAG)

As a web accessibility consultant, I advise public and private companies on how to make their digital solutions accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. This includes both technical advice and implementation of WCAG standards.

Additive Manufacturing for the Visually Impaired

Working to develop and improve methods for creating tactile 3D-printed materials for teaching and orientation for the blind and visually impaired. This project combines the latest 3D printing technologies with special educational needs.

Master’s Degree in ICT and Learning

Immersing myself in how information and communication technology can be integrated into learning activities through my ongoing master’s degree at Aalborg University.
