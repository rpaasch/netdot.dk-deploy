About Me

Rasmus Paasch

I am a specialist in digital technologies with a particular focus on accessibility, education, and leadership. With a background as a teacher and deep technical insight, I combine pedagogy and technology in my work.

Professional Background

I currently work as an ICT consultant at IBOS - The Institute for the Blind and Partially Sighted in Hellerup, Denmark, where I assess assistive technologies for visually impaired citizens. I have previously been a project manager on international Erasmus+ projects on tactile 3D-printed teaching materials and Virtual Reality for the visually impaired.

My career also includes:

  • Acting Department Head at Langelinieskolen (2020-2021)
  • Publishing Editor at Clio Online ApS (2014-2015)
  • Teacher specializing in mathematics, science, and IT guidance (2005-2020)
  • Partner in a digital agency focusing on custom web solutions (2015-2019)

Education

  • Currently studying Master in ICT and Learning (MIL) at Aalborg University
  • Educated as a teacher from Vordingborg Seminarium with a bachelor project in “ICT and learning in mathematics education”
  • PD modules in Multimedia, Learning & Learning Resources, Media Pedagogical Guidance
  • Leadership training and union representative training

Technical Skills

  • Web: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, SQL, WCAG accessibility
  • Programming languages: Python, Ruby
  • System administration: Windows, Linux, Mac
  • Maker technologies: 3D printing, prototyping
  • Network technologies

Personal

Born in 1980 on the island of Møn, Denmark, father of two girls and residing in Dyssegård. In my spare time, I enjoy spending time with my family, reading, programming, listening to jazz records, brewing beer, running, and nerding out with coffee.
